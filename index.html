About Me

Hon. Rayyan Hussein

I am honored to serve as the Chairman of Kaduna South Local Government Area, bringing with me over 15 years of experience in public service and community development.

My vision for Kaduna South is one of sustainable growth, where every citizen has access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. I believe in governance that is transparent, inclusive, and accountable to the people.

Through this blog, I aim to share updates on our projects, insights into local governance, and my personal reflections on serving our community. Your feedback and engagement are always welcome.
